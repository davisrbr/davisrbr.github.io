I do research in the Math of Machine Learning group at PNNL, where so far I've researched interpretability, the science of deep learning, and robustness.

I help organize the (hybrid!) Pacific Northwest Seminar on Topology, Algebra, and Geometry in Data Science at the University of Washington.

I've previously worked on a few different start-ups. I'm also a Robert Caro fan, into EA, and have been recently thinking about what philosophers have to say about concepts (currently reading this book on philosophy and connectionism).
